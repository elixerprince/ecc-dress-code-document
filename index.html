DRESS CODE – EVENING and WEEKEND STUDENTS

It is expected that the majority of evening students who will take classes are working individuals. Work attire is appropriate mode of dress for these students. Weekend College students are expected to attire themselves in a manner appropriate for the office.

All students should ensure all these requirements are met at the start of the Semester.

Kindly make every effort to take your ID photograph in the dress code of your Department.
